<p><p>The sweeping car market is comprised of three main types of sweepers: mechanical broom, regenerative-air, and vacuum sweepers. Mechanical broom sweepers rely on rotating brushes to collect debris and are ideal for heavy-duty cleaning on rough surfaces. Regenerative-air sweepers use a vacuum and a high-pressure air system to collect dust and dirt effectively, making them suitable for urban environments. Vacuum sweepers operate by creating suction to collect debris and are commonly used in parking lots and streets. Each type of sweeper offers unique features and advantages depending on the specific cleaning needs.</p></p>
