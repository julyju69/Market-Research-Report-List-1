<p><p>The corrugated pharmaceutical packaging market is categorized into three types based on the number of layers: single-wall, double-wall, and triple-wall. Single-wall packaging consists of a single layer of corrugated board, providing basic protection. Double-wall packaging has two layers, offering improved durability and protection for more fragile pharmaceutical products. Triple-wall packaging, with three layers, provides maximum strength and protection, making it suitable for heavy or sensitive pharmaceuticals. These different types of packaging options cater to pharmaceutical companies' diverse needs for secure and reliable transportation of their products.</p></p>
