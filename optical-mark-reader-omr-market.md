<p><p>An Optical Mark Reader (OMR) is a device used to capture data from marked fields on paper forms or documents. The market for OMR devices can be divided into two types: semi-automatic and automatic. In the semi-automatic market, users manually feed the paper forms into the reader, which then scans and captures the data. In the automatic market, the OMR devices are integrated into a larger automated system, where the forms are automatically fed, scanned, and the data is captured without any manual intervention.</p></p>
