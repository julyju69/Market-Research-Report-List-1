<p><p>DC & DC switching regulators are electronic devices used to convert a DC input voltage into a regulated DC output voltage. There are four main types of switching regulators on the market. The Buck regulator steps down the input voltage, Boost regulator steps up the input voltage, Buck/Boost regulator can either step up or step down the input voltage, and the Invert regulator generates a negative output voltage. Each of these regulators serves different purposes and can be used in various applications to meet specific power requirements.</p></p>
