On-premise market: This refers to the software that is installed and operated on the user's local servers and infrastructure. This model offers higher data security but requires significant upfront investment and ongoing maintenance costs.</p><p>2. On-cloud market: In this model, the software is hosted and managed by a third-party provider on the internet. Users access the software remotely through a web browser.
