<p><p>The cable tray market encompasses various types. Ladder type cable tray is widely used and consists of two side rails connected by rungs, offering flexibility and ease of installation. Tray type cable tray has a solid bottom with side rails and is suitable for applications requiring extra protection. Large span cable tray is designed for longer distances, providing support without requiring additional columns. Combined type cable tray combines the features of both ladder and tray types, offering versatility and flexibility to meet different installation requirements.</p></p>
