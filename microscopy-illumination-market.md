<p><p>Microscopy illumination market is categorized into fixed and mobile types. Fixed microscopy illumination refers to a system that remains in a fixed position in a laboratory or research setting. It is typically integrated into the microscope and provides consistent illumination conditions. On the other hand, mobile microscopy illumination refers to a portable system that can be easily moved and used in various locations. It offers flexibility and convenience for field researchers or those who require microscopy analysis on the go. Both fixed and mobile illumination setups play a vital role in enabling high-quality imaging and analysis in various scientific applications.</p></p>
