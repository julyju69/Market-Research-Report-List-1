<p><p>Centrifuges with containment systems are used in various industries for separating liquids and solids. The market is categorized into two types: vertical and horizontal. Vertical centrifuges are designed with a vertical axis of rotation, allowing for efficient separation of materials and easy discharge of solids. They are widely used in industries such as pharmaceuticals, biotechnology, and chemical processing. On the other hand, horizontal centrifuges have a horizontal axis of rotation, offering better stability and higher capacity for processing larger volumes. They are commonly used in industries like oil and gas, food and beverage, and wastewater treatment.</p></p>
