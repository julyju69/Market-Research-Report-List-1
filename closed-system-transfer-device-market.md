<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Closed System Transfer Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Closed Vial Access Devices</li><li>Closed Syringe Safety Devices</li><li>Closed Bag/Line Access Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Closed System Transfer Device (CSTD) market comprises three main types: Closed Vial Access Devices (CVADs), Closed Syringe Safety Devices (CSSDs), and Closed Bag/Line Access Devices (CBADs). CVADs facilitate the safe transfer of drug vials by sealing the vial's septum during withdrawal. CSSDs are designed to prevent needlestick injuries by enclosing the needle and syringe after use. CBADs are used for closed transfer of drugs from a bag to an administration set or vice versa, minimizing contamination risks. These devices promote safer drug handling and administration, reducing the risk of exposure for healthcare workers.</p></p>
