<p><p>The bike lights and reflectors market consists of two main types: headlights and reflectors. Headlights are lighting devices installed on the front of bicycles to illuminate the road ahead, enhancing visibility for the rider and alerting others of their presence. Reflectors, on the other hand, are passive devices made of reflective materials strategically placed on bicycles. They reflect light back to its source, increasing visibility in low-light conditions and improving safety. Both headlights and reflectors play a crucial role in ensuring the safety of cyclists and preventing accidents.</p></p>
