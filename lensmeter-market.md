<p><p>Lensmeters are devices used in optometry to measure optical power and prescription of lenses. There are two main market types of lensmeters: manual lensmeters and automatic lensmeters. Manual lensmeters require manual adjustments and measurements, with operators manually rotating dials and making observations. Automatic lensmeters, on the other hand, utilize automated technology and advanced algorithms to provide more accurate and efficient measurements. Automatic lensmeters offer features like auto-focus, auto-alignment, and computerized readings that enhance accuracy and reduce human error.</p></p>
