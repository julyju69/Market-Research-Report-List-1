<p><p>Single lumen microcatheters are used in various medical procedures for delivery, diagnosis, aspiration, and steerable purposes. In delivery applications, the microcatheters are utilized to deliver medication or other substances to specific locations within the body. For diagnosis, they are used to guide imaging tools for better visualization and accurate diagnosis. In aspiration procedures, they are employed to remove fluid or tissue samples from the body. Lastly, in steerable applications, the microcatheters can be navigated through complex pathways within the body for precise treatment.</p></p>
