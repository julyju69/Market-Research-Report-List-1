<p><p>2.5D IC flip chip products are advanced integrated circuit packages used in electronic devices. The market types for these products include Copper Pillar, Solder Bumping, Tin-lead eutectic solder, Lead-free solder, Gold Bumping, and Others. Copper Pillar uses copper pillars for interconnecting chips, providing high signal density and thermal performance. Solder Bumping involves attaching chips using solder bumps, enabling fine pitch interconnects. Tin-lead eutectic solder utilizes a mixture of tin and lead for chip connections. Lead-free solder replaces lead with alternative materials to reduce environmental impact. Gold Bumping uses gold bumps for reliable chip connections. "Others" refer to alternative flip chip technologies outside these categories.</p></p>
