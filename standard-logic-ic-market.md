<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Standard Logic IC Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>TTL IC (Transistor-transistor Logic)</li><li>CMOS Logic IC (Complementary Metal Oxide Semiconductor)</li><li>BiCMOS Logic IC (Bipolar CMOS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Standard logic ICs include TTL ICs, CMOS logic ICs, and BiCMOS logic ICs. TTL ICs, or Transistor-Transistor Logic, use bipolar junction transistors and are known for their high-speed performance and robustness. CMOS logic ICs, or Complementary Metal-Oxide Semiconductor, use both NMOS and PMOS transistors and are popular due to their low power consumption and high noise immunity. BiCMOS logic ICs combine TTL and CMOS technologies, integrating both bipolar and CMOS transistors for improved performance and power characteristics while maintaining compatibility with TTL and CMOS devices.</p></p>
