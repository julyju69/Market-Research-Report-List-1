<p><p>The 4K portable projector market can be divided into three types: LCD, DLP, and LCOS. LCD projectors use liquid crystal display technology to project images and are known for their vibrant colors and sharp image quality. DLP projectors use Digital Light Processing technology, which produces high contrast ratios and smooth motion, making them ideal for fast-paced visuals. LCOS projectors, on the other hand, use Liquid Crystal on Silicon technology to offer deep black levels and excellent color accuracy. Each type has its own advantages and is chosen based on specific needs and preferences.</p></p>
