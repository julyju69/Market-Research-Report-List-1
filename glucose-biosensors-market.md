<p><ul><li>Self-monitoring of Blood Glucose (SMBG)</li><li>Continuous Glucose Monitoring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The glucose biosensors market consists of two main types: Self-monitoring of Blood Glucose (SMBG) and Continuous Glucose Monitoring (CGM) systems. The SMBG market refers to the devices used by individuals to monitor their blood glucose levels on their own, usually through finger-prick tests. On the other hand, the CGM market includes wearable sensors that continuously measure glucose levels in the interstitial fluid, providing real-time data and alerts for better diabetes management. Both types of biosensors play a crucial role in diabetes care and can help individuals make informed decisions about their diet, exercise, and medication.</p></p>
