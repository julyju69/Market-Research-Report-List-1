<p><p>Medical surgical lasers are categorized into different types based on the medium used for generating the laser beam. CO2 lasers are commonly used for procedures such as skin resurfacing and tumor removal. Argon lasers are used for eye surgeries and treating skin conditions. Nd:YAG lasers are used for various procedures including hair removal and tattoo removal. Diode lasers are commonly used for dental procedures. Other types of lasers include Erbium lasers, which are used for dermatological procedures. Each type of laser has specific applications and benefits in the medical field.</p></p>
