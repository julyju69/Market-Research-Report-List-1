<p><p>A lift platform, also known as a scissor lift, is a hydraulic-powered equipment used to raise or lower workers and materials to different heights. <p><p>There are different types of lift platforms available in the market. Fixed type lift platforms are designed to stay in a fixed location and provide vertical movement for loading and unloading heavy goods. On the other hand, mobile type lift platforms are movable and can be transported to different locations as needed. They are commonly used for tasks that require flexibility and agility, such as maintenance work on elevated structures or in warehouses where goods need to be lifted and transported within the facility. Both types offer solutions for vertical movement, but differ in terms of mobility and usage.</p></p>
