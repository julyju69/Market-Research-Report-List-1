<p><p>The pet genetic test market offers various types of tests including saliva and stool tests. This helps in predicting potential health issues, breed composition, and even behavioral characteristics. On the other hand, stool tests involve analyzing a pet's feces for genetic markers that can provide insights into their digestive health, food sensitivities, and potential diseases.
