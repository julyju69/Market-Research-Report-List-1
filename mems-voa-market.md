<p><p>MEMS VOA, also known as MEMS Variable Optical Attenuator, is a device used in fiber-optic telecommunications to control the power levels of incoming light signals. The increasing demand for high-speed data transmission and the expanding fiber-optic network infrastructure are key drivers of market growth. These devices are used for precise and accurate control of light intensity in various medical applications, including optical coherence tomography (OCT) systems, DNA sequencers, and fluorescence microscopy.</p><p>The latest trend observed in the MEMS VOA market is the development of miniaturized, high-performance devices. Manufacturers are focusing on reducing the size, weight, and power consumption of MEMS VOA, making them suitable for integration into compact optical modules used in communication systems.
