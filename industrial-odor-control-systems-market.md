<p><p>Industrial Odor Control Systems market includes three types of systems:</p><p>1. Physical Odor Control System: This type of system uses physical barriers like activated carbon filters or scrubbers to remove or neutralize odors. These systems physically trap or absorb odorous molecules, preventing them from spreading.</p><p>2. Chemical Odor Control System: This system involves the use of chemicals to eliminate odors. It includes processes like oxidation, absorption, or chemical reactions to neutralize the odor-causing compounds.</p><p>3. Biological Odor Control System: This system uses biological processes such as microbial digestion to break down odor-causing compounds, converting them into less odorous or non-odorous substances.</p></p>
