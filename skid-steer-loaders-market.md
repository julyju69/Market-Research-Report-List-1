<p><p>Skid steer loaders are versatile construction machines designed for various tasks. There are two main types: wheeled skid steer loaders and tracked skid steer loaders. Wheeled skid steer loaders have wheels, providing better maneuverability on hard surfaces and are ideal for indoor work or on concrete. Tracked skid steer loaders have tracks, allowing better traction on rough or muddy terrain, making them suitable for outdoor use. Both types come with attachments, such as buckets, pallet forks, or sweepers, to handle different materials and perform diverse tasks efficiently.</p></p>
