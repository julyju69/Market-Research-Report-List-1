<p><p>Study tools can be categorized into two market types: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise study tools refer to software that is installed and operated on local computers or servers, requiring manual maintenance and updates by users. Conversely, cloud-based study tools are accessed through the internet, stored and maintained on remote servers, eliminating the need for manual updates. Cloud-based tools offer the convenience of accessibility from any device with internet connection, while on-premise tools provide users with control over their data and infrastructure.</p></p>
