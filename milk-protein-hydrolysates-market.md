<p><p>The milk protein hydrolysates market is divided into two main types: whey protein hydrolysates and casein protein hydrolysates. Whey protein hydrolysates are produced by breaking down whey protein into smaller fragments, which makes it easier for the body to digest and absorb. They are commonly used in sports nutrition products and infant formulas. On the other hand, casein protein hydrolysates are derived from casein, a slow-digesting protein. They are used in various dietary supplements aimed at promoting muscle recovery and growth.</p></p>
