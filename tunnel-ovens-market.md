<p><p>Tunnel ovens are specialized baking devices used in the food industry. They are divided into various types based on their heating methods and features. Air Impingement Tunnel Ovens use high-speed jets of hot air to quickly cook food. Cyclotherm Tunnel Ovens circulate heat through radiant tubes. Deck Tunnel Ovens have multiple levels or decks for baking different products simultaneously. Direct Heated Tunnel Ovens use direct heat sources like gas or oil burners. The "Others" category includes ovens with different features such as steam injection or hybrid heating methods.</p></p>
