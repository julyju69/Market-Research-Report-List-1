<p><p>Substation automation refers to the use of advanced technologies in managing and controlling electrical substations. Transmission substations refer to facilities that support the transmission of electricity from power plants to distribution substations, which then deliver power to end-users. Distribution substations, on the other hand, are responsible for receiving high voltage electricity from transmission substations and transforming it to a lower voltage suitable for local distribution. Both transmission and distribution substations can benefit from automation by improving efficiency, reliability, and monitoring capabilities of the electrical grid.</p></p>
