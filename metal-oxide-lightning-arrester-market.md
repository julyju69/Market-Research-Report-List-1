<p><p>Metal oxide lightning arresters, also known as surge arresters, are devices used to protect electrical equipment from lightning-induced power surges. There are two main types of metal oxide lightning arresters in the market: Polymer MOA and Porcelain MOA.</p><p>Polymer MOA refers to lightning arresters with a polymer housing. They are lightweight, compact, and suitable for outdoor applications. They offer high energy absorption capabilities and excellent reliability.</p><p>On the other hand, Porcelain MOA lightning arresters have a housing made from porcelain. They are known for their robust construction and strength, making them suitable for high-voltage applications. They provide effective protection against lightning strikes and power surges.</p><p>Both types of lightning arresters are valuable assets in preventing damage to electrical systems caused by lightning and are commonly used in various industries.</p></p>
