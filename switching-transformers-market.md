<p><p>Switching transformers are used in power electronic circuits to step up or step down voltage levels. Different market types of switching transformers are available based on their configurations. Push/Pull switching transformers use a pair of transistors to alternately switch the current on and off. Half bridge switching transformers have two transistors and provide a higher power output. Full bridge switching transformers use four transistors and are capable of handling even higher power. These transformers are essential in various applications such as power supplies, inverters, and converters to efficiently regulate voltage levels.</p></p>
