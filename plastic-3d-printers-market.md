<p><p>Plastic 3D printers are classified into different types based on the printing technology and the materials used. One type is FDM (Fused Deposition Modeling) thermoplastic, which involves the extrusion of a molten plastic filament to build the object layer by layer. Another type is Polyjet photopolymer resin, where liquid resin is dispensed and cured using ultraviolet light to create the desired object. Both FDM thermoplastic and Polyjet photopolymer resin are popular in the market due to their ability to create complex and detailed 3D printed objects.</p></p>
