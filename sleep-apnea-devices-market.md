<p><strong>The Sleep Apnea Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Therapeutic Devices</li><li>Diagnostic Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sleep apnea devices market can be categorized into two main types: therapeutic devices and diagnostic devices. Therapeutic devices are used to treat sleep apnea by assisting patients in breathing during sleep. These devices include continuous positive airway pressure (CPAP) machines, which deliver a constant flow of air to keep airways open. Diagnostic devices, on the other hand, are used to diagnose sleep apnea by monitoring various parameters during sleep, such as oxygen levels, breathing patterns, and heart rate. These devices aid in identifying the severity of the condition and determining the appropriate treatment plan.</p></p>
