<p><p>The Car Digital Video Recorder market consists of two types: Single Channel and Multi-Channel. Single Channel recorders monitor and record video footage from one camera, typically positioned on the dashboard or windshield. They are commonly used for personal vehicles. On the other hand, Multi-Channel recorders can simultaneously capture video footage from multiple cameras, offering comprehensive coverage of the surroundings. These are usually employed in commercial vehicles, taxis, or fleets, as they provide a more comprehensive view of the road and surroundings for security and surveillance purposes.</p></p>
