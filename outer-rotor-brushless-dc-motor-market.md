<p><p>The outer rotor brushless DC motor market can be categorized into two types: with sensor and without sensor. This allows for more precise control over the motor's speed and torque, making them suitable for applications that require high levels of accuracy. On the other hand, "without sensor" motors do not have built-in position sensors but rely on external control systems to determine the rotor position. These motors are generally more cost-effective and find application in various industries.</p></p>
