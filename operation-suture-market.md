<p><strong>The Operation Suture Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absorbable Sutures</li><li>Non-absorbable Sutures</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The market for surgical sutures can be broadly classified into two types: absorbable sutures and non-absorbable sutures. Absorbable sutures are made from special materials that break down over time and are designed to be absorbed by the body. They are commonly used in internal surgical procedures where the sutures do not need to be removed. Non-absorbable sutures, on the other hand, are made from materials that are not broken down by the body and need to be removed manually. They are often used in external surgeries and in situations where long-term wound support is required.</p></p>
