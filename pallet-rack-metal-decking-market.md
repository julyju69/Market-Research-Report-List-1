<p><p>The Pallet Rack Metal Decking Market offers various types of decking options for pallet racks. Standard U-Channel Decks have a U-shaped channel that supports the decking. Inverted Type Decks have a flange on top to prevent items from sliding off. Flush Flat Decks are flat and do not have any edges or flanges. Inside Waterfall Decks have a lip on the front and back to hold items in place. Flared Type Decks have flared edges for additional stability. Other types include specialized decking options such as wire mesh decks or perforated decks, offering unique features and benefits.</p></p>
