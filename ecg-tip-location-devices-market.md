<p><p>The ECG Tip Location Devices market consists of two main types: ECG Tip Confirmation and ECG with Magnetic Tracking. ECG Tip Confirmation devices are used to determine the correct position of the tip of an ECG catheter during cardiac procedures, ensuring accurate readings and minimizing complications. ECG with Magnetic Tracking devices, on the other hand, utilize magnets to track the movement and location of the catheter tip in real-time, providing improved navigational guidance for healthcare professionals during catheterization procedures. Both types of devices aim to enhance the precision and efficiency of cardiovascular interventions.</p></p>
