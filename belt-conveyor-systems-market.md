<p><p>Belt conveyor systems are classified into two main types: open belt conveyor systems and enclosed belt conveyor systems. Open belt conveyor systems consist of an uncovered belt loop supported by rollers, used for transporting bulk materials within industries such as mining and agriculture. On the other hand, enclosed belt conveyor systems are covered with a protective housing, preventing materials from spilling or contamination. These systems are commonly employed in manufacturing and food processing industries to ensure clean and safe transportation of goods.</p></p>
