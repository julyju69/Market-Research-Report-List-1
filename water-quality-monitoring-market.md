<p><p>The water quality monitoring market consists of various types of equipment used to analyze the quality of water. TOC analyzers measure total organic carbon in water samples, while pH meters determine the acidity or alkalinity of the water. Dissolved oxygen analyzers measure the amount of oxygen dissolved in water, and conductivity sensors measure the water's ability to conduct electrical currents. Turbidity meters assess the cloudiness or clarity of the water. There are also other types of equipment available in the market that can be used for water quality monitoring purposes.</p></p>
