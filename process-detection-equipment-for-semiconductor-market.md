<p><p>Process detection equipment for the semiconductor market includes types such as front track detection and back track detection. Front track detection refers to the equipment used for monitoring and detecting any anomalies or issues occurring during the manufacturing process of semiconductors. It focuses on the initial stages of production. On the other hand, back track detection involves the monitoring of the final stages of semiconductor production, ensuring that any defects or errors are identified before the completion of the process. Both types of detection equipment play a crucial role in maintaining the quality and reliability of semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
