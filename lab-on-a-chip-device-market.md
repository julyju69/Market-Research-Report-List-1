<p><p>A lab-on-a-chip device, also known as a microfluidic device, is a miniaturized laboratory system that integrates multiple laboratory functions onto a single small chip. These devices offer benefits such as reduced sample and reagent volumes, faster analysis times, portability, and ease of use.</p><p>The lab-on-a-chip device market has been experiencing significant growth in recent years and is expected to continue expanding.
