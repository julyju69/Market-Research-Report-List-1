<p><p>The Remote I & O Devices Market encompasses three types: Compact I/O, Field I/O, and Modular I/O. Compact I/O refers to small-sized input and output devices designed for limited applications. Field I/O devices serve as a bridge between the control system and field devices like sensors and actuators. They facilitate data exchange between these devices and the control system. Modular I/O devices offer expansive and flexible input and output options, capable of accommodating a wide range of field devices and configurations.</p></p>
