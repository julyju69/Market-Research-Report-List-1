<p><p>Handheld data loggers are electronic devices used to collect and store data from various sensors or instruments. Single channel loggers can record data from one sensor or input, such as temperature or pressure. Dual channel loggers have the capability to record data from two different inputs simultaneously. Multi-channel loggers offer the ability to collect data from multiple sensors or inputs at once, providing a more comprehensive data collection solution. These different types cater to different needs, allowing users to choose the logger that best fits their specific data logging requirements.</p></p>
