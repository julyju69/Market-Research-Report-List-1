Liquid flour improver is in a liquid form and is convenient to mix with water or other liquid ingredients. It is commonly used in large-scale production. Granule flour improver is in a granular form, often used in small-scale baking or specialty applications. These market types provide various options to cater to different baking requirements.</p></p>
