<p><p>The AC-DC medical power supply market is classified into two types: external AC-DC medical power supply and internal AC-DC medical power supply. </p><p>The external AC-DC medical power supply refers to power supplies that are designed as separate units and used outside of the medical equipment. They are commonly used in portable medical devices and provide the necessary power for operation.</p><p>On the other hand, the internal AC-DC medical power supply is integrated directly within the medical equipment. These power supplies are installed inside the equipment and are responsible for converting the alternating current (AC) from the power source into direct current (DC) that is required by the device to function properly.</p></p>
