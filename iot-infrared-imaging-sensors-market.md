<p><strong>The IoT Infrared Imaging Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Short Wave Infrared Imaging</li><li>Medium Wave Infrared Imaging</li><li>Long Wave Infrared Imaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The IoT Infrared Imaging Sensors market comprises three main types: Short Wave Infrared Imaging, Medium Wave Infrared Imaging, and Long Wave Infrared Imaging. Short Wave Infrared Imaging sensors operate in the wavelength range of 0.9 to 1.7 µm, offering high resolution and accurate inspections. Medium Wave Infrared Imaging sensors operate in the wavelength range of 1.7 to 3 µm, detecting heat that is not visible to the naked eye. Long Wave Infrared Imaging sensors operate in the wavelength range of 8 to 14 µm, providing improved thermal resolution for enhanced surveillance and industrial applications.</p></p>
