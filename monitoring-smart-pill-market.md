Temperature monitoring smart pills measure and report the internal body temperature, aiding in the monitoring of fever or hypothermia. Pressure monitoring smart pills help assess and manage conditions like bowel disorders, as they measure and evaluate the pressure changes in the digestive system. These market types of smart pills provide valuable insights for healthcare professionals and patients alike.</p></p>
