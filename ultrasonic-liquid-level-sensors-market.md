<p><p>Ultrasonic liquid level sensors are used for measuring the level of liquids in various applications. Direct level measurements involve the sensor being in direct contact with the liquid and emitting ultrasonic waves to measure the distance. This type is suitable for environments with clean liquids. Indirect level measurements involve the ultrasonic waves being transmitted through a medium, such as a solid rod or tube, to measure the liquid level. This type is useful for measuring corrosive or hazardous liquids, as the sensor is not directly exposed to them.</p></p>
