<p><p>The Holographic Diffraction Grating market includes two main types: Plane Type Holographic Grating and Concave Type Holographic Grating. Plane Type Holographic Grating is a flat sheet with evenly spaced lines which diffract light to create a unique interference pattern. It is commonly used in spectroscopy and telecommunications. On the other hand, Concave Type Holographic Grating is curved with lines arranged in a concave shape. This type offers higher diffraction efficiency and is primarily used in high-end applications such as laser pulse compression and astronomy.</p></p>
