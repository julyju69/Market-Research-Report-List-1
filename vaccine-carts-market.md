<p><p>The Vaccine Carts market consists of three main types: Refrigerated Vaccine Carts, Frozen Vaccine Carts, and Uncooled Vaccine Carts. Refrigerated Vaccine Carts are designed to maintain a cool temperature range for vaccines that need to be stored at a specific temperature. Frozen Vaccine Carts, on the other hand, are used to store vaccines that require freezing temperatures. Lastly, Uncooled Vaccine Carts do not require any specific temperature control and are commonly used for administering vaccines in mobile clinics or remote locations where electricity is not readily available.</p></p>
