<p><p>The automotive millimeter-wave radar IC market is categorized into three types: GaAs, SiGe BiCMOS, and RF CMOS. GaAs technology utilizes gallium arsenide, which offers high power and efficiency, making it suitable for long-range radar applications. SiGe BiCMOS combines silicon and germanium materials, enabling high-performance radar ICs with low power consumption. RF CMOS technology uses complementary metal-oxide-semiconductor materials, providing cost-effective and highly integrated solutions for short-range radar systems. Each type has its advantages and capabilities, allowing for diverse options in the automotive millimeter-wave radar IC market.</p></p>
