<p><p>Worm gear reducers refer to a mechanical transmission system used for reducing motor speed and increasing torque output. Different types of worm gear reducers exist in the market. Orthogonal worm gear reducers are designed for transmitting motion at a 90-degree angle, making them suitable for applications with limited space. Parallel-shaft worm gear reducers offer a compact design with the input and output shafts aligned. Coaxial worm gear reducers have the input and output shafts positioned on the same axis, making them ideal for applications requiring inline transmission. Each type caters to specific needs in various industries.</p></p>
