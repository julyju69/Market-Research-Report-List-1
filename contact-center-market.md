<p><p>Contact center market can be classified into two types: on-premise and cloud-based solutions. On-premise contact centers involve the installation and management of physical infrastructure on-site, allowing organizations to have complete control and customization. In contrast, cloud-based contact centers offer a flexible and scalable solution hosted on the internet, eliminating the need for physical infrastructure and providing easy access for remote agents. This type allows businesses to rapidly deploy contact center services without significant upfront costs, offering greater agility and cost-efficiency.</p></p>
