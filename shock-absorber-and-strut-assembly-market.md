<p><strong>The Shock Absorber and Strut Assembly Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Twin-tube</li><li>Mono-tube</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The shock absorber and strut assembly market consists of two main types: twin-tube and mono-tube. Twin-tube shock absorbers have an inner and outer tube, with hydraulic fluid flowing between them to dampen and control the vehicle's suspension movement. They are cost-effective and widely used in passenger cars. On the other hand, mono-tube shock absorbers have a single tube where gas and hydraulic fluid are separated by a piston. They offer better heat dissipation, improved performance, and are typically used in high-performance vehicles.</p></p>
