<p><p>The hydraulic bolting tool market consists of various types, including wrenches and torque pumps. A hydraulic wrench is a tool that uses hydraulic pressure to apply torque to fasten or loosen bolts. It is commonly used in industries requiring precision, such as manufacturing and construction. On the other hand, a torque pump is a device that provides the necessary hydraulic pressure to operate hydraulic wrenches effectively. It pumps hydraulic fluid into the wrench, allowing it to generate torque and perform the desired bolting operations with ease.</p></p>
