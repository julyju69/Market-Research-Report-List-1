<p><p>Lead-acid batteries refer to a type of rechargeable battery commonly used for various applications. There are two main market types for lead-acid batteries - VRLA (Valve Regulated Lead Acid) and flooded batteries. VRLA batteries are sealed, maintenance-free batteries that utilize a valve to regulate gas pressure, making them suitable for sealed enclosures and sensitive environments. On the other hand, flooded batteries require regular maintenance, as they need to be refilled with water periodically, making them more suitable for applications where cost-effectiveness is prioritized over maintenance convenience.</p></p>
