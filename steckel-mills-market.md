<p><p>Steckel Mills are used in the production of rolled steel sheets and strips. They come in different market types, such as Single Stand and Twin Stand. In a Single Stand market, there is only one roll stand involved in the process, which helps in achieving thinner and wider sheets. On the other hand, a Twin Stand market involves two roll stands working side-by-side, allowing for higher productivity and the ability to produce thicker and narrower strips. Both market types offer unique advantages and suit different production needs in the steel industry.</p></p>
