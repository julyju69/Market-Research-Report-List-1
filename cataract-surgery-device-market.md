These lasers are also being used for corneal incisions, capsulotomies, and lens fragmentation, reducing the dependence on manual techniques and improving the overall surgical experience.</p><p>Another trend is the shift towards premium IOLs. <p><strong>The Cataract Surgery Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Intraocular lens (IOL)</li><li>Ophthalmic Viscoelastic Device (OVD)</li><li>Phacoemulsification Equipment</li><li>Femtosecond Laser Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cataract surgery device market is divided into different types of equipment used during the surgery. Intraocular lens (IOL) is a type of artificial lens that is implanted in the eye after the natural lens is removed. Ophthalmic Viscoelastic Device (OVD) is a gel-like substance used to maintain the shape of the eye during surgery. Phacoemulsification equipment is used to break up the cataract within the eye using ultrasound waves. Femtosecond laser equipment uses laser technology to perform precise incisions and fragmentation of the lens during surgery.</p></p>
