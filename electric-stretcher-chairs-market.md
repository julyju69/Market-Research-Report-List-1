<p><p>The electric stretcher chairs market can be classified into two types - general stretcher chairs and special stretcher chairs. General stretcher chairs are designed to cater to the needs of the general population and are commonly found in hospitals and healthcare facilities. They provide basic functionalities such as adjustable headrests and leg sections. On the other hand, special stretcher chairs are specifically designed to cater to specific needs such as bariatric patients or patients requiring intensive care. These chairs offer additional features like wider seats, higher weight capacity, and advanced positioning options.</p></p>
