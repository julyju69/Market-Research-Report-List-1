<p><p>Solar lawn lights are outdoor lighting fixtures that are powered by solar energy. They are designed to provide illumination to lawns, gardens, pathways, and other outdoor areas. These lights typically consist of a solar panel, a battery, a light source, and a controller. During the day, the solar panel absorbs sunlight and converts it into electrical energy, which charges the battery. As soon as it gets dark, the controller activates the light source, which illuminates the surrounding area.</p><p>The solar lawn light market has been experiencing steady growth in recent years. This growth can be attributed to several factors, including the increasing emphasis on energy conservation and the rising popularity of environmentally-friendly products. Solar lawn lights offer numerous advantages over traditional lighting options, such as reduced electricity consumption and lower maintenance costs.
