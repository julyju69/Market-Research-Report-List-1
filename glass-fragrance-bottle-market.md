<p><p>The glass fragrance bottle market consists of various types, including roller ball bottles and spray bottles. Roller ball bottles have a small ball applicator at the top that allows users to roll the fragrance onto their skin. These bottles are convenient for targeted application and are often used for oil-based fragrances. On the other hand, spray bottles have a nozzle that releases a fine mist of fragrance when pressed. They are suitable for liquid-based fragrances and are preferred for wider and more even distribution on the body. Both bottle types cater to different preferences and usage needs in the fragrance market.</p></p>
