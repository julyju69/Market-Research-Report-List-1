<p><p>The Commercial IC Card Electricity Smart Meter market consists of two main types: Single Phase and Three Phase. </p><p>Single Phase smart meters are designed for smaller commercial establishments with lower electricity consumption. These meters measure the flow of electricity through a single wire and are capable of handling single-phase circuits.</p><p>In contrast, Three Phase smart meters are used in larger commercial buildings that have higher electricity demands. These meters measure the flow of electricity through three wires and can handle three-phase circuits.</p><p>Both types of meters are equipped with IC card technology, allowing for easy payment and monitoring of electricity usage in commercial settings.</p></p>
