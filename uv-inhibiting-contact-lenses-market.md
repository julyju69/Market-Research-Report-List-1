The market for these lenses is divided into three types based on the specific range of UV radiation they protect against. UV-C radiation refers to the shortest wavelength and highest energy ultraviolet radiation, which is mostly absorbed by the earth's atmosphere; UV-A radiation represents the longer wavelength and lower energy UV radiation that reaches the earth's surface; and UV-B radiation falls in between UV-C and UV-A, having moderate energy and capability to partly penetrate the atmosphere.</p></p>
