<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The laser mirror substrate market consists of three main types: flat, convex, and concave. Flat mirrors have a smooth surface that reflects light with minimal distortion, making them ideal for precise laser applications. Convex mirrors bulge outward, causing the reflected light to spread out, making them suitable for wide-angle or long-distance laser devices. On the other hand, concave mirrors curve inward, focusing the reflected light to a specific point, making them useful for laser systems that require concentrated beams or precise optical alignment.</p></p>
