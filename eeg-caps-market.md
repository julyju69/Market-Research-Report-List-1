<p><p>EEG caps, also known as electroencephalography caps, are wearable devices used to measure and record the electrical activity of the brain. These caps are equipped with multiple electrodes that are placed on the scalp to detect brain signals. EEG caps are widely used in clinical research, diagnostics, and brain-computer interface applications.</p><p>The EEG caps market is witnessing significant growth due to the increasing prevalence of neurological disorders such as epilepsy, sleep disorders, and brain injuries. The demand for reducing the invasiveness and improving the accuracy of brain signal measurement is driving the adoption of EEG caps. Additionally, advancements in technology, such as wireless EEG caps and dry electrodes, are further bolstering market growth.</p><p>Furthermore, the integration of artificial intelligence and machine learning algorithms with EEG caps has expanded their applications in the field of neurofeedback therapy, mental health monitoring, and brain-computer interfaces. This has created lucrative opportunities for market players and stimulated research and development activities in the EEG caps market.</p><p>Moreover, the growing geriatric population, rising healthcare expenditure, and increasing awareness about brain disorders are contributing to the market growth. However, the high cost of EEG caps and the lack of skilled professionals to interpret and analyze EEG signals may hinder market growth.</p><p>In conclusion, the EEG caps market is expected to grow at a robust CAGR of 12.80% during the forecast period. Advancements in technology, increasing prevalence of neurological disorders, and expanding applications of EEG caps are the key factors driving market growth.
