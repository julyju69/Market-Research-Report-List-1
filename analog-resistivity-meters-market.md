<p><p>Analog resistivity meters are available in two main types: surface resistivity meters and volume resistivity meters. Surface resistivity meters are used to measure the resistance of a material's surface to the flow of electric current, providing valuable information about its conductivity. Volume resistivity meters, on the other hand, measure the resistance throughout the entire volume of a material, giving insight into its ability to conduct electricity internally. Both types offer analog readings, providing users with visual indications of resistivity levels.</p></p>
