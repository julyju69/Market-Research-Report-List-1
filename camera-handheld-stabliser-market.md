<p><p>The Camera Handheld Stabiliser market offers various types of stabilizers, including 3-axis and 4-axis stabilizers. A 3-axis stabilizer provides stabilization along three rotational axes: pitch, roll, and yaw. It helps create smooth and steady footage by minimizing camera shakes and movements in these directions. On the other hand, a 4-axis stabilizer adds an additional axis, typically known as the vertical axis or the "Z-axis," which aids in stabilizing up and down movements. Apart from these, the market may also include other types such as 2-axis stabilizers or innovative designs with unique features, catering to different user preferences and requirements.</p></p>
