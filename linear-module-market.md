<p><p>The linear module market consists of different types of modules such as ball screw linear modules and synchronous belt linear modules. Ball screw linear modules utilize a threaded shaft and ball bearings to convert rotary motion into linear motion, providing precise and efficient movement. On the other hand, synchronous belt linear modules use a toothed belt and pulleys to achieve linear motion, offering a cost-effective and durable solution. Both types have their own advantages and applications in various industries, ranging from manufacturing to automation.</p></p>
