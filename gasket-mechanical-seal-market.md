<p><p>The gasket market is divided into two main types: metallic and non-metallic. Metallic gaskets are typically made from materials like stainless steel or aluminum and are preferred for high-pressure applications due to their strength and ability to withstand extreme temperatures. Non-metallic gaskets, on the other hand, are made from materials such as rubber, cork, or graphite and are commonly used for lower pressure applications where flexibility and sealing capability are prioritized. Both types cater to different needs and industries, providing sealing solutions for various mechanical equipment and systems.</p></p>
