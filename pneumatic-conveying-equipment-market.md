<p><p>Pneumatic conveying equipment refers to systems used to transport bulk materials through a pipeline using air pressure. There are three main types of pneumatic conveying systems: positive pressure conveying, vacuum pressure conveying, and combined conveying. </p><p>Positive pressure conveying involves using air pressure to push materials through the pipeline. Vacuum pressure conveying, on the other hand, operates by creating a vacuum to suck materials into the pipeline. Lastly, combined conveying combines positive and vacuum pressure methods for efficient transportation of bulk materials. These systems are widely used in industries such as food processing, pharmaceuticals, and manufacturing.</p></p>
