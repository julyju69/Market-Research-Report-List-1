<p><p>The handheld golf rangefinder market can be categorized into three types: laser golf rangefinders, GPS golf rangefinders, and others. Laser golf rangefinders use lasers to calculate the distance between the user and a target, providing accurate measurements. GPS golf rangefinders, on the other hand, utilize global positioning system technology to determine distances, displaying them on a digital screen. Lastly, others refer to any technology that falls outside of these two categories, such as rangefinders that use both laser and GPS technology or those that incorporate additional features like slope calculation or hazard detection.</p></p>
