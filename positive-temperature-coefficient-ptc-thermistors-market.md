<p><p>Positive Temperature Coefficient (PTC) thermistors are used for various applications and are divided into two main types: Conductive Polymer Positive Temperature Coefficient (CPTC) and Polymer Positive Temperature Coefficient (PPTC) thermistors. </p><p>CPTC thermistors are made of conductive polymers that exhibit an increase in resistance with temperature, providing protection against overcurrents. They are commonly used in electronic circuits to prevent device damage.</p><p>PPTC thermistors are made of polymer materials and have a similar function to CPTC thermistors. However, PPTC thermistors have a higher resistance and slower response, making them suitable for applications requiring overcurrent protection.</p></p>
