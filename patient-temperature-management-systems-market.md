<p><strong>The Patient Temperature Management Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Patient Warming Devices</li><li>Patient Cooling Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Patient temperature management systems include patient warming devices and patient cooling devices. Patient warming devices are used to maintain the body temperature of patients during surgeries, preventing hypothermia and reducing the risk of surgical site infections. These devices include warming blankets, warming mattresses, and forced-air warming systems. On the other hand, patient cooling devices are utilized to lower the body temperature of patients, mostly in cases of fever, hyperthermia, or cardiac arrest. Cooling blankets, cooling caps, and intravascular cooling systems are examples of patient cooling devices.</p></p>
