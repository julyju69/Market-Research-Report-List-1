<p><strong>The Optical Fiber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Mode Optical Fiber</li><li>Multimode Optical Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical fiber market is categorized into two types - single mode optical fiber and multimode optical fiber. Single mode optical fiber is designed to allow only one mode of light to propagate at a time, ensuring longer transmission distances and higher bandwidth. It is commonly used in long-distance communication applications. On the other hand, multimode optical fiber allows multiple modes of light to propagate simultaneously, offering lower transmission distances but lower costs. It is often used in short-distance applications such as local area networks. Both market types cater to different requirements in terms of distance, bandwidth, and cost.</p></p>
