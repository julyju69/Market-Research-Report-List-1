<p><strong>The Artificial Larynx Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Neck Type</li><li>Intraoral Type</li><li>Electromyography (EMG) Transduce</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Artificial Larynx market includes three types: Neck Type, Intraoral Type, and Electromyography (EMG) Transduce. The Neck Type is a wearable device that produces sound externally by placing it on the neck. The Intraoral Type is a device used to create sound by vibrating against the oral cavity walls. The Electromyography (EMG) Transduce type uses electrical signals from muscles to generate sound. These artificial larynx types serve as alternative speech production methods for individuals with laryngectomy or vocal disabilities.</p></p>
