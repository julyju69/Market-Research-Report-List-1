<p><p>The laser focusing lens market comprises different types of lenses, including plano-convex and meniscus lenses. Plano-convex lenses have a flat surface on one side and a curved surface on the other, allowing them to converge laser beams to a focal point. Meniscus lenses, on the other hand, have a concave surface on one side and a convex surface on the other, which helps in focusing and reducing aberrations in the laser beam. These lenses are commonly used in various industries for laser cutting, engraving, and scientific research purposes.</p></p>
