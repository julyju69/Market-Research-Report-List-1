<p><p>The magnets and magnetic materials market can be divided into three main types: semi-hard magnets, soft magnets, and permanent/hard magnets. Semi-hard magnets possess unique properties that lie between soft and hard magnets, making them suitable for various applications like televisions and electric appliances. Soft magnets have low magnetic properties and are easily magnetized and demagnetized, making them ideal for transformers and inductor cores. Permanent/hard magnets retain their magnetism and are widely used in industries such as automotive, electronics, and energy generation due to their strong magnetic properties and resistance to demagnetization.</p></p>
