<p><p>A welding dust collector is a device used to extract and filter airborne particles generated during welding processes to improve air quality and ensure a safe working environment. There are two main types of welding dust collectors: portable and fixed. Portable dust collectors are compact and can be easily moved around in a workshop or job site. They provide flexibility and are suitable for small-scale welding operations. Fixed dust collectors, on the other hand, are permanently installed in a designated area and are best suited for large-scale welding operations where a centralized system is required for effective dust collection.</p></p>
