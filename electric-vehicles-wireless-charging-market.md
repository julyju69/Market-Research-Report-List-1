<p><ul><li>Electromagnetic Induction</li><li>Magnetic Resonance</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric vehicle wireless charging market comprises two main types: electromagnetic induction and magnetic resonance. Electromagnetic induction involves transferring energy wirelessly between a charging pad on the ground and a receiver pad underneath the electric vehicle, through the use of a magnetic field. Magnetic resonance, on the other hand, uses resonance phenomena to transfer energy wirelessly between two magnetic coils. Both types allow for convenient and efficient charging of electric vehicles without the need for physical connections, increasing the convenience and usability of electric vehicles.</p></p>
