<p><p>The thermal imaging software market includes two types: cloud-based and web-based software. Cloud-based thermal imaging software operates on remote servers, allowing users to access and use the software via the internet. It offers flexibility, scalability, and easy updates, making it suitable for companies of all sizes. In contrast, web-based thermal imaging software is accessed through a web browser and installed on the user's device.
