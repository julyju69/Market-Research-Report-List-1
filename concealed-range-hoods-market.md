<p><p>Concealed range hoods are kitchen appliances used to remove smoke, fumes, and odors while cooking. They come in two main market types: axial flow and centrifugal. Axial flow range hoods have a straight-through airflow design, where the air is drawn in from one end and expelled from the other. On the other hand, centrifugal range hoods use a curved blower wheel to create a centrifugal force that draws the air in and expels it from the side. Both types have their advantages and can be chosen based on individual preferences and requirements.</p></p>
