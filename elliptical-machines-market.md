<p><p>The elliptical machines market consists of three main types: elliptical trainer, elliptical cross-trainer, and elliptical glider. Elliptical trainers are stationary machines that simulate jogging or running without causing excessive strain on joints. Elliptical cross-trainers combine the benefits of elliptical trainers with upper body workout options, typically incorporating movable handlebars. Elliptical gliders, on the other hand, focus solely on lower body movements, providing a low-impact cardiovascular workout. These different types cater to varying preferences and fitness goals, offering options for individuals seeking a comprehensive workout or specific target areas.</p></p>
