<p><p>The Coal Water Slurry (CWS) market can be categorized into different types based on the concentration of coal in the slurry. High Concentration CWS refers to a mixture with a high proportion of coal particles, usually above 65% by weight. Medium Concentration CWS contains a moderate amount of coal, generally ranging from 45% to 65% by weight. Other types in the market include low concentration CWS, ultra-high concentration CWS, and super-high concentration CWS, which respectively indicate lower or higher coal proportions than high concentration CWS. These categories define the varying compositions of coal and water in the market.</p></p>
