<p><p>Autoclave bags are specially designed bags used for the collection and disposal of biohazardous waste. These bags are used in healthcare facilities, laboratories, and industries to safely dispose of medical waste that may be contaminated with harmful microorganisms. Autoclave bags are made of polypropylene material, which can withstand high temperatures and pressure during the autoclaving process, ensuring the complete sterilization of the waste.</p><p>The autoclave bags market is witnessing significant growth due to the increasing awareness about safe waste disposal, strict government regulations regarding medical and biohazardous waste management, and the rising number of healthcare facilities worldwide. The market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.20% during the forecast period.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the autoclave bags market is the development of bio-based and biodegradable autoclave bags. These bags are made of eco-friendly materials such as paper, cornstarch, and bamboo, which can be easily decomposed and do not harm the environment. The growing focus on sustainability and the increasing demand for eco-friendly products are driving the adoption of bio-based autoclave bags.</p><p>Another trend in the market is the integration of smart features in autoclave bags.
