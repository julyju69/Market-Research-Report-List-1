<p><p>The Biomass Gasifier Market includes two main types: Fixed-Bed Type Gasifier and Fluidized-Bed Type Gasifier. The Fixed-Bed Type Gasifier is characterized by a stationary bed of biomass where the gasification process occurs. This type is suitable for solid biomass fuel and offers simplicity and stability. On the other hand, the Fluidized-Bed Type Gasifier features a bed of biomass particles suspended in a fluidized state, resulting in better mixing and enhanced conversion rates. This type is more versatile, capable of processing various biomass types, and provides higher gas yields.</p></p>
