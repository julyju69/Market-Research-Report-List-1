<p><p>The High Purity Synthetic Quartz Glass Market is segmented into two types: Chemical Vapor Deposition (CVD) and Plasma Deposition. CVD involves depositing a thin layer of quartz onto a substrate using a chemical process, resulting in high-quality quartz with high purity levels. This method allows for precise control over the deposition process and is commonly used in the fabrication of semiconductor devices. On the other hand, Plasma Deposition utilizes plasma to deposit quartz on a substrate, enabling high deposition rates and efficient production of quartz glass products.</p></p>
