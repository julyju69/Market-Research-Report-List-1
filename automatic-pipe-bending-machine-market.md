<p><p>The automatic pipe bending machine market can be categorized into two types: fully automatic pipe bending machines and semi-automatic pipe bending machines. Fully automatic machines require minimal human intervention, as they can perform tasks such as loading material, bending, and unloading the finished product automatically. On the other hand, semi-automatic machines require some level of manual operation, where operators need to load and unload the pipes manually, while the bending process is automated. Both types offer efficient and precise bending solutions for various industries.</p></p>
