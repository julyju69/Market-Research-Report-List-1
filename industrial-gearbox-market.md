<p><strong>The Industrial Gearbox Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-stage Gearbox</li><li>Multi-stage Gearbox</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial gearbox market can be categorized into two types: single-stage gearbox and multi-stage gearbox. A single-stage gearbox consists of one set of gears that transmit power from the input shaft to the output shaft with a specific gear ratio. This type of gearbox is suitable for applications that require low gear ratios. On the other hand, a multi-stage gearbox consists of multiple sets of gears arranged in series that enable higher gear ratios. It is commonly used in applications where higher torque and speed reduction are required.</p></p>
