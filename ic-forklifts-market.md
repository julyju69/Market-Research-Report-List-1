<p><p>IC forklifts, also known as internal combustion forklifts, are widely used in various industries to lift and transport heavy loads. There are three main types of IC forklifts based on the type of fuel they use: gasoline, diesel, and liquid propane. Gasoline forklifts are efficient and suitable for both indoor and outdoor applications. Diesel forklifts provide higher torque and are more durable, making them ideal for heavy-duty tasks. Liquid propane forklifts are cleaner and emit fewer emissions, making them suitable for indoor use and environmentally sensitive areas.</p></p>
