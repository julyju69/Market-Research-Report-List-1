<p><ul><li>Open Nozzles</li><li>Closed Nozzles</li></ul></p>
<p><p>High Pressure Water Mist Systems are categorized into two types: Open Nozzles and Closed Nozzles. Open Nozzles release water mist in an open area, covering a larger space. They are commonly used in outdoor settings or areas with high ceilings. On the other hand, Closed Nozzles release water mist in closed or confined spaces, such as server rooms or electrical cabinets. Closed Nozzles provide a more targeted and localized fire suppression solution. Both types of systems are designed to extinguish fires using high-pressure water mist technology.</p></p>
