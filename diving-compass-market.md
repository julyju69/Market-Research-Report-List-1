<p><p>Diving compass market can be categorized into two types: digital compass and pointer compass. A digital compass utilizes modern technology, such as sensors and microprocessors, to provide accurate and precise readings of direction. It often comes with additional features like backlighting and tilt compensation. On the other hand, a pointer compass is a traditional compass that relies on a magnetic needle to indicate direction. It is simple to use and does not require any power source. The choice between the two types depends on the diver's preference and need for advanced functionality.</p></p>
