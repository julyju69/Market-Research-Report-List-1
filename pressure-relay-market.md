<p><strong>The Pressure Relay Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Plunger</li><li>Diaphragm</li><li>Spring tube</li><li>Bellows</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pressure relay market types include plunger, diaphragm, spring tube, and bellows. Plunger pressure relays rely on the movement of a plunger to generate a pressure signal. Diaphragm pressure relays use a flexible diaphragm to detect pressure changes. Spring tube pressure relays consist of a coiled tube that expands or contracts in response to pressure variations. Bellows pressure relays utilize a metal bellows that expands or contracts under pressure. These different types of pressure relays effectively monitor and control pressure variations in various industrial applications.</p></p>
