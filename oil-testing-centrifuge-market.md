<p><p>The oil testing centrifuge market can be classified into two main types: desktop and portable. Desktop centrifuges are larger, stationary devices that are commonly found in laboratories or testing facilities. They have a higher capacity and can process larger volumes of oil samples at once. On the other hand, portable centrifuges are smaller and more compact, designed for on-site or field testing purposes.
