<p><p>The chromatography instruments market can be categorized into different types, including gas chromatography instruments, liquid chromatography, supercritical fluid chromatography, thin-layer chromatography, and others. Gas chromatography instruments are used to separate and analyze volatile compounds. Liquid chromatography separates compounds using a liquid mobile phase. Supercritical fluid chromatography utilizes supercritical fluids as the mobile phase. Thin-layer chromatography separates compounds on a thin layer of adsorbent material. The "others" category encompasses various chromatography techniques, such as ion exchange chromatography and size exclusion chromatography, for specific applications in different industries.</p></p>
