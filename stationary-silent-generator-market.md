<p><p>A stationary silent generator, also known as a standby generator or backup generator, is a device that generates electricity when the main power supply fails. Unlike portable generators, which can be moved easily, stationary silent generators are permanently installed and are designed to provide a reliable and continuous power source for residential, commercial, and industrial applications.</p><p>These generators are typically used in places where power outages are frequent, such as hospitals, data centers, and manufacturing facilities. They provide a seamless transition from the main power supply to the backup generator, ensuring uninterrupted power and preventing any disruption to critical operations.</p><p>The global stationary silent generator market has been witnessing steady growth in recent years. The market is driven by various factors such as increasing demand for reliable power supply, growing frequency of power outages, and rapid industrialization. <p><p>The stationary silent generator market comprises two main types: low power and high power.
