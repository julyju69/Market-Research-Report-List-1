<p><ul><li>Pretreatment</li><li>Cell Assembly</li><li>Post Processing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lithium-ion battery equipment market consists of three main types: pretreatment, cell assembly, and post-processing. Pretreatment refers to the process of preparing the required materials for the battery production, such as mixing and coating the active materials on the electrode. Cell assembly involves the construction of the battery by stacking the positive and negative electrodes with a separator and electrolyte in between. Post-processing refers to the final steps of battery production, including formation, testing, and finishing touches like packing and labeling. These three types cover the entire process of lithium-ion battery manufacturing.</p></p>
