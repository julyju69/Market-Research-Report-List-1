<p><p>The commercial booster pump market consists of two main types: single stage and multiple stages. Single stage booster pumps are designed to increase water pressure by a single impeller, while multiple stage booster pumps use multiple impellers to gradually increase pressure. Single stage pumps are typically used for smaller applications with moderate pressure needs, while multiple stage pumps are more suitable for larger projects that require high pressure. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining adequate water pressure in commercial settings.</p></p>
