<p><p>The Solar Automatic Tracking System Market includes two main types: Single Axis Solar Automatic Tracking System and Dual Axis Solar Automatic Tracking System. The Single Axis system is designed to track the movement of the sun on a single plane, usually from east to west. It is cost-effective and suitable for small-scale solar installations. On the other hand, the Dual Axis system can track the sun's movement both horizontally and vertically, providing the highest efficiency by capturing maximum solar radiation. It is generally utilized for large-scale solar power generation projects.</p></p>
