<p><p>Nursing home beds are available in two types: fixed height and liftable height. Fixed height beds are set at a specific level, providing stability and preventing accidents for patients who may have difficulty getting in or out of bed. Liftable height beds, on the other hand, can be adjusted to different heights, making it easier for caregivers to attend to patients' needs and for patients to enter or exit the bed. These different market types cater to varying needs in nursing home facilities.</p></p>
