<p><p>The Connected Home Security System market can be divided into two main types: Monitor System and Alarm System. A Monitor System involves the use of surveillance cameras and sensors to monitor the home premises, allowing homeowners to remotely view and record activities in real-time. On the other hand, an Alarm System comprises sensors and detectors that trigger loud alarms or send alerts to homeowners and monitoring centers in case of any suspicious activities or breaches. Both types aim to enhance home security by providing surveillance, deterrence, and rapid response to potential threats.</p></p>
