<p><p>Ground Engaging Tools (GET) refer to the equipment used for excavation and earthmoving operations. The GET market encompasses various types, including bucket teeth, blades, and cutting edges. Bucket teeth are tooth-like attachments fitted on excavator and loader buckets to dig and scoop materials. Blades, on the other hand, are used for grading and leveling surfaces. Cutting edges are flat blades that attach to the edges of buckets and blades to enhance cutting performance. These tools are essential for efficient and effective earthmoving and construction activities.</p></p>
