<p><p>Solid-State Photomultipliers (SSPM) are photodetectors that convert light into electrical signals. They are available in two main market types: Monocoque Type and Array Type. The Monocoque Type SSPMs consist of a single detection unit that provides high photon detection efficiency and low dark count. They are more suitable for applications requiring high sensitivity. On the other hand, Array Type SSPMs consist of an array of individual detection units integrated on a single chip, offering higher spatial resolution and larger active areas. These are commonly used for applications requiring high timing resolution and imaging capabilities.</p></p>
