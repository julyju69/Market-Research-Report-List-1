<p><p>Local SEO software market can be categorized into two types: on-premises and cloud-based. On-premises software is installed and operates on the user's local computer or servers, requiring maintenance, upgrades, and security management by the user. In contrast, cloud-based software operates on remote servers and is accessed through the internet. It eliminates the need for installation, providing convenience, scalability, and automatic updates. Cloud-based software offers accessibility from anywhere at any time, while on-premises software offers more control and security but requires greater technical expertise and infrastructure maintenance.</p></p>
