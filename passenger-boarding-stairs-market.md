<p><p>Passenger boarding stairs refer to equipment used to allow passengers to board and disembark from aircraft. There are two main types of passenger boarding stairs: towable passenger stairways and chassis mounted passenger stairways. Towable passenger stairways are mobile stairs that can be attached to a towing vehicle and moved around the airport, providing flexibility and ease of use. Chassis mounted passenger stairways, on the other hand, are permanently mounted on a vehicle chassis, offering stability and durability. Both types play a crucial role in facilitating safe and efficient passenger boarding processes at airports.</p></p>
