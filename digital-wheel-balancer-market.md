<p><p>The Digital Wheel Balancer market consists of two main types: Manual Digital Wheel Balancer and Automatic Digital Wheel Balancer. </p><p>In the Manual Digital Wheel Balancer market, the balancing process is carried out by human operators. They manually input the necessary information, such as wheel dimensions, and perform the required adjustments to achieve balance.</p><p>On the other hand, the Automatic Digital Wheel Balancer market involves advanced technology that automates the process. These balancers are equipped with sensors and software that automatically measure wheel dimensions, detect imbalances, and make necessary adjustments without human intervention.</p><p>Both types of balancers aim to ensure optimal wheel balance, but the manual variant requires human expertise and effort, while the automatic variant offers increased efficiency and convenience.</p></p>
