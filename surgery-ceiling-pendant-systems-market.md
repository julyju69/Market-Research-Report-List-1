<p><p>Surgery ceiling pendant systems are used in operating rooms to provide support and organization for medical equipment. There are four main types of these systems: Fixed pendants are stationary and cannot be moved or adjusted. Fixed retractable pendants have the ability to retract or extend to accommodate different needs. Single arm movable pendants can move in a single direction to reach different areas. Double multi-arm movable pendants have multiple arms that can move in various directions, providing greater flexibility and access to equipment during surgeries.</p></p>
