<p><p>The anti-snoring devices market consists of three main types: ventilator type, silicone utensils type, and electronic biological type. Ventilator type devices improve airflow through the nose and throat to reduce snoring. Silicone utensils type devices are typically placed in the mouth to adjust the position of the jaw and tongue, keeping the airway open during sleep. Electronic biological type devices use sensors to detect snoring and deliver gentle vibrations or stimuli to encourage sleep position changes. These devices aim to alleviate snoring and improve sleep quality for individuals affected by snoring issues.</p></p>
