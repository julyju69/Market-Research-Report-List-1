<p><p>Hemodialysis concentrates refer to solutions used in hemodialysis machines to prepare dialysate, which is the fluid that helps remove waste and excess fluids from the blood during the dialysis process. There are two types of hemodialysis concentrates: acid concentrates and bicarbonate concentrates. Acid concentrates contain chemicals (such as acetic and citric acid) to lower the pH of the dialysate, while bicarbonate concentrates use sodium bicarbonate to ensure the pH is within a normal range. These concentrates play a crucial role in maintaining the balance of electrolytes and pH levels during hemodialysis.</p></p>
