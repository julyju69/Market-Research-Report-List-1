<p><p>The air and gas leak detectors market comprises three types of detectors. Firstly, portable detectors are lightweight and handheld devices used for detecting leaks in various locations. Secondly, fixed detectors are stationary devices installed in specific areas to constantly monitor for leaks. These detectors are often connected to alarm systems and control panels for immediate action. Lastly, detector tubes consist of glass tubes filled with a chemical reagent that changes color when exposed to a specific gas, providing a simple and quick method for identifying gas leaks.</p></p>
