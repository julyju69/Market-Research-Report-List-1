<p><p>Desiccated coconut powder is available in two market types: pure and mixed. Pure desiccated coconut powder is made solely from dried and finely ground coconut flesh. It does not contain any additives or additional ingredients. On the other hand, mixed desiccated coconut powder is prepared by blending the coconut flesh with other substances such as sugar or milk powder. This type is often used in confectioneries or as a cooking ingredient to enhance the flavor and improve the texture of various dishes. Both pure and mixed desiccated coconut powder offer different options to cater to various consumer preferences and applications.</p></p>
