<p><ul><li>Dehydrated Dried Soup</li><li>Instant Dried Soup</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dried soup is available in two main market types: dehydrated dried soup and instant dried soup. Dehydrated dried soup is made by removing the moisture content from fresh ingredients, resulting in a longer shelf life and concentrated flavors. It requires simmering in water or broth for a longer time before consuming. On the other hand, instant dried soup is designed for quick and convenient preparation, often requiring only hot water for rehydration. It is pre-cooked and typically contains smaller, easily dissolvable ingredients. Both types offer a convenient option for individuals looking for a time-saving meal.</p></p>
