<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The Berthing Aid System market comprises two types: Laser-Based and GPS-Based systems. Laser-Based systems employ laser technology to accurately measure distances between vessels and dock, making berthing procedures easier and safer. These systems typically use laser range finders or scanners to determine the precise position and distance from the dock. On the other hand, GPS-Based systems use global positioning technology for positioning and navigation, providing real-time data to assist in berthing operations. These systems utilize advanced GPS receivers and software to ensure precise positioning and maneuvering of vessels during docking.</p></p>
