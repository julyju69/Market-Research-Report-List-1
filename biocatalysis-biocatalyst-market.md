The Biocatalyst market consists of different types such as Hydrolases, Oxidoreductases, Transferases, and others. Hydrolases catalyze hydrolysis reactions, Oxidoreductases facilitate oxidation-reduction reactions, and Transferases aid in transferring functional groups between molecules. Other types of biocatalysts may include lyases, ligases, and isomerases. Each type of biocatalyst plays a crucial role in promoting sustainable and efficient processes in industries such as pharmaceuticals, food production, and biofuels.</p></p>
