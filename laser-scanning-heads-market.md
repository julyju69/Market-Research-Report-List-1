<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The Laser Scanning Heads market can be categorized into four types: Two-Axis, Three-Axis, Five-Axis, and Others. Two-Axis scanning heads are capable of moving lasers in two directions, allowing them to scan objects horizontally and vertically. Three-Axis scanning heads possess an additional axis that enables them to rotate the laser beam, providing enhanced flexibility for scanning complex surfaces. Five-Axis scanning heads offer even greater flexibility by adding two more axes of movement, enabling intricate scanning of three-dimensional objects. Lastly, the "Others" category includes scanning heads with different configurations or specifications not covered by the previous types.</p></p>
