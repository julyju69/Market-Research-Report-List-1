<p><p>The Transcatheter Pacing System market includes different types of conditions such as Atrioventricular Block, Sinus Node Dysfunction, Atrial Fibrillation, and Others. Atrioventricular Block refers to a disruption in the electrical signals between the heart's upper and lower chambers, leading to an irregular heart rhythm. Sinus Node Dysfunction involves a malfunctioning sinus node, which is responsible for initiating the heart's electrical signals. Atrial Fibrillation is an irregular heartbeat caused by abnormal electrical signals in the heart's upper chambers. Lastly, the "Others" category encompasses various heart rhythm disorders not specifically mentioned above.</p></p>
