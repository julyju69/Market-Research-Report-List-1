<p><p>The Ultraviolet (UV) Sensor market comprises three main types: UVA Sensor, UVB Sensor, and UVC Sensor. UVA sensors detect long-wave ultraviolet light with wavelengths between 315 nm and 400 nm. UVB sensors identify medium-wave ultraviolet light ranging from 280 nm to 315 nm, while UVC sensors measure short-wave ultraviolet light within the range of 100 nm to 280 nm. These sensors are used in various applications, including skin monitoring, water purification, UV curing, and germicidal systems, to monitor and control exposure to different levels of ultraviolet radiation.</p></p>
