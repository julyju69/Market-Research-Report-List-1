<p><p>Swing barriers are security devices used to control access to certain areas. There are two main types of swing barriers in the market: manual and electric. Manual swing barriers require physical effort to operate, usually by pushing or pulling them open and closed. They are a cost-effective option for areas with low traffic and limited security needs. On the other hand, electric swing barriers are automated and powered by electricity. They offer added convenience and security features like sensors and control systems, making them suitable for high-traffic areas and locations that require stricter access control.</p></p>
