<p><p>The Acute Heart Failure (AHF) Therapeutics market encompasses various types of treatments. B-Blockers are medications that help regulate heart rate and blood pressure. Calcium Channel Blockers relax blood vessels, reducing strain on the heart. Cardiac Glycosides improve heart function by strengthening contractions. Diuretics remove excess fluid from the body, relieving fluid buildup in the lungs and other organs. Morphine is used for pain relief and reducing anxiety in AHF patients. Vasodilators/Nitrates dilate blood vessels, making it easier for the heart to pump blood. Other AHF treatments may include blood thinners and antiplatelet drugs.</p></p>
