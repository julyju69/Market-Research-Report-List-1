<p><p>The Volumetric Dosing Dispenser market comprises two main types: automatic and manual. Automatic dosing dispensers are designed to accurately measure and dispense predetermined volumes of liquid or solid substances, eliminating the risk of human error. These dispensers often have programmable features and are commonly used in industries such as pharmaceuticals, food and beverages, and chemicals. On the other hand, manual dosing dispensers require human control and supervision throughout the dosing process. These dispensers are usually simpler in design and find application in small-scale operations or situations where precision and automation may not be necessary.</p></p>
