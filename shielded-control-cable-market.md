<p><p>Shielded control cables are used to transmit and control electrical signals in various applications. Single conductor control cables consist of a single copper conductor insulated with a protective material. These cables are suitable for low voltage applications and provide excellent signal transmission. On the other hand, multi-conductor control cables possess multiple insulated conductors, allowing for the transmission of multiple signals simultaneously. They are commonly used in applications where multiple control signals need to be transmitted within a single cable, such as industrial automation systems.</p></p>
