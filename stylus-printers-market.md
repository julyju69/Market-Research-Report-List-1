<p><ul><li>Ethernet Stylus Printers</li><li>USB Stylus Printers</li><li>Wireless Stylus Printers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Stylus printers come in different market types based on their connectivity options. Ethernet stylus printers are designed to connect directly to a network via an Ethernet cable, allowing multiple users to access the printer simultaneously. USB stylus printers connect directly to a computer or device using a USB cable, offering a more straightforward and reliable connection. Wireless stylus printers use WiFi or Bluetooth technology to connect to devices without the need for cables, offering convenience and flexibility in printer placement.</p></p>
