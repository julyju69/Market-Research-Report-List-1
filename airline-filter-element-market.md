<p><p>The airline filter element market consists of various types such as photocatalyst, activated carbon, electrostatic precipitator, negative ions, ozone, and others. Photocatalyst filters use light to break down pollutants. Activated carbon filters absorb and remove impurities. Electrostatic precipitators use an electric charge to trap particles. Negative ion filters release negatively charged ions to neutralize pollutants. Ozone filters produce ozone to eliminate contaminants. Other types of filters may include HEPA filters or UV filters. These filters help improve air quality by removing or neutralizing harmful substances in the air.</p></p>
