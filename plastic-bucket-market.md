<p><p>The plastic bucket market consists of two main types, namely High-Density Polyethylene (HDPE) and Polypropylene (PP). HDPE buckets are known for their strong and durable nature, making them ideal for carrying heavy materials. They are commonly used in industries such as agriculture, construction, and chemicals. On the other hand, PP buckets are lighter in weight and offer good resistance to chemicals and moisture. They are often found in industries like food and beverage, pharmaceuticals, and household cleaning. Both HDPE and PP buckets cater to different market needs based on their unique characteristics.</p></p>
