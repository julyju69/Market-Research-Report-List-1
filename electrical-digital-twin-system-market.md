<p><ul><li>Parts Twin</li><li>Product Twin</li><li>Process Twin</li><li>System Twin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electrical Digital Twin System Market consists of four main types: Parts Twin, Product Twin, Process Twin, and System Twin. </p><p>Parts Twin focuses on creating digital replicas of individual components within an electrical system. Product Twin involves creating a virtual model of the entire product, including its electrical features. Process Twin focuses on simulating and optimizing the manufacturing process of electrical products. System Twin encompasses the entire electrical system, providing a comprehensive digital replica that can be used for analysis and optimization purposes. Each type plays a vital role in the overall digital twin ecosystem for electrical systems.</p></p>
