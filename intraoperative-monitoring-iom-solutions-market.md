<p><p>Intraoperative Monitoring (IOM) Solutions refer to the techniques used during surgery to monitor the function of the nervous system. There are different types of IOM solutions available, including Electroencephalography (EEG), which measures the electrical activity of the brain; Electromyography (EMG), which measures the electrical activity of muscles; and Evoked Potentials, which assess the brain's response to external stimuli. Other types of IOM solutions may include monitoring of nerve conduction, spinal cord function, and motor evoked potentials. These techniques help ensure the safety and integrity of the nervous system during surgical procedures.</p></p>
