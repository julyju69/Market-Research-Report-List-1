<p><ul><li>Wireless Control Type</li><li>Remote Control Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cabin Management Systems (CMS) are advanced technological solutions used in aircraft to enhance passenger comfort and convenience. The market for CMS can be categorized into two types: Wireless Control Type and Remote Control Type. </p><p>In the Wireless Control Type, passengers can access and control various cabin features and settings through wireless devices like smartphones or tablets. This offers seamless and personalized control over in-flight entertainment, lighting, temperature, and other amenities. </p><p>On the other hand, the Remote Control Type allows cabin crew members to manage and monitor the entire cabin environment through a centralized control system. This includes controlling lighting, audio-video systems, passenger service units, and other functions from a designated control panel. These systems enable efficient and coordinated management of the cabin, ensuring a smooth and enjoyable flight experience for passengers.</p></p>
