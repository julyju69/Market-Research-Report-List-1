<p><strong>The Dental Ultrasonic Scaler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Magnetostrictive Ultrasonic Scalers</li><li>Piezoelectric Ultrasonic Scalers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dental Ultrasonic Scaler market is comprised of two main types: Magnetostrictive Ultrasonic Scalers and Piezoelectric Ultrasonic Scalers. Magnetostrictive Ultrasonic Scalers use a magnetostrictive transducer that produces ultrasonic vibrations through metal stacks. These scalers have a wider range of power settings and offer effective subgingival calculus removal. On the other hand, Piezoelectric Ultrasonic Scalers use piezoelectric crystals to generate ultrasonic vibrations, providing greater precision and control during dental procedures. They are generally preferred for supragingival scaling and delicate dental treatments.</p></p>
