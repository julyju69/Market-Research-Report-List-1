<p><p>Thick film heaters are a type of heating element that can be divided into two main markets: metal thick film heaters and ceramic thick film heaters. Metal thick film heaters are composed of a metal substrate, which provides good thermal conductivity and rapid heat transfer. Ceramic thick film heaters, on the other hand, are made of a ceramic substrate, which offers excellent electrical insulation and temperature stability. Both types of thick film heaters are widely used in various industries for applications such as heating liquids, gases, and solid surfaces.</p></p>
