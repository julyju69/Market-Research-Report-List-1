<p><p>The hospital elevator market consists of two main types: automatic hospital elevators and semi-automatic hospital elevators. Automatic hospital elevators are equipped with advanced technology and require minimal human intervention. They can be operated by simply pressing a button to select the desired floor. On the other hand, semi-automatic hospital elevators are partially automated and may require some manual intervention, such as closing the doors manually before the elevator starts moving. Both types serve the purpose of transporting patients, medical equipment, and staff efficiently within the hospital premises.</p></p>
