<p><p>Micro supercapacitors are energy storage devices that are composed of thin film electrodes made from nanostructured materials. These electrodes are designed in such a way that they form in-plane interdigital configurations, allowing for efficient charge storage and retrieval. Additionally, there are micro supercapacitors available in the market that utilize three-dimensional (3D) electrodes. These 3D electrodes provide a larger surface area for increased energy density. Overall, these different types of micro supercapacitors offer unique advantages and applications in various industries.</p></p>
