QDEF, or Quantum Dot Enhancement Film, is a type of QLED where a thin layer of quantum dots is applied to a conventional LCD display, improving the color gamut and overall picture quality. QLED market refers to the broader market for QLED displays, which includes both QDEF and other types of QLED technologies, such as QD-OLED (Quantum Dot Organic Light-Emitting Diode) displays.</p></p>
