<p><p>The SIP Based IP PBX (Private Branch Exchange) market includes two main types: Cloud-hosted and On-Site (Premise). In the Cloud-hosted market, businesses access PBX services remotely through the internet, with the service provider handling all infrastructure and maintenance. This offers scalability and flexibility to businesses with limited IT resources. On the other hand, the On-Site market involves businesses maintaining and managing their own PBX hardware and software on their premises. This type provides more control and customization options but requires greater investment and technical expertise.</p></p>
