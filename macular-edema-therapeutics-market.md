<p><p>The macular edema therapeutics market involves different types of treatments for this condition. Laser treatment uses a focused beam of light to treat abnormal blood vessels in the eye. Anti-VEGF injection involves injecting medications that can block the formation of new blood vessels and reduce leakage. Anti-inflammatory treatment targets inflammation that causes macular edema. Vitrectomy is a surgical procedure that removes the vitreous gel from the eye to alleviate symptoms. These treatment options aim to alleviate the swelling and restore normal vision in patients with macular edema.</p></p>
