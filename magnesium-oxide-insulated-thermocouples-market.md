<p><strong>The Magnesium Oxide Insulated Thermocouples Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Grounded Type</li><li>Ungrounded Type</li><li>Exposed Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Magnesium oxide insulated thermocouples can be classified into three types based on their construction and application: grounded type, ungrounded type, and exposed type. Grounded type thermocouples have one thermoelement grounded to the probe sheath, providing faster and more accurate temperature readings. Ungrounded type thermocouples have both thermoelements insulated from the probe sheath, offering galvanic isolation and preventing ground loop interference. Exposed type thermocouples have the thermoelements directly exposed to the environment, making them suitable for high-temperature measurements and rapid response applications. Each type has its distinct advantages and is used in various industrial processes.</p></p>
