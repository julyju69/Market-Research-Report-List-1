<p><ul><li>Geological Logging Machine</li><li>Comprehensive Logging Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The well logging equipment market comprises two main types: Geological Logging Machines and Comprehensive Logging Machines. Geological Logging Machines are used to measure and analyze the geological properties of a well, such as rock formations and fluid compositions. These machines provide valuable information for exploration and production purposes. On the other hand, Comprehensive Logging Machines gather a wider range of data, including geological, petrophysical, and engineering parameters. This type of equipment helps in understanding the overall well conditions, optimizing reservoir management, and making informed decisions regarding drilling and production operations.</p></p>
