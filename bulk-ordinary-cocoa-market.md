<p><p>Bulk (ordinary) cocoa, also known as commodity cocoa, refers to the market types associated with cocoa liquor, cocoa butter, and cocoa powder. Cocoa liquor is the result of grinding cocoa beans into a paste and is used as a base ingredient in chocolate production. Cocoa butter is the fat obtained from the cocoa bean and is widely used in the cosmetics and food industries. Lastly, cocoa powder is produced by removing the cocoa butter from the cocoa liquor, resulting in a dry and powdered form that is commonly used in baking and as a beverage.</p></p>
