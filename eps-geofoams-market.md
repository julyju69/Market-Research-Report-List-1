<p><p>EPS Geofoams are categorized into two main types based on their physical properties: softness and hardness. Soft EPS Geofoams are used in applications where cushioning and impact absorption are required, such as in sports fields and playgrounds. Hard EPS Geofoams, on the other hand, are utilized in projects that require structural support and load-bearing capabilities, like road embankments and foundations. The choice between soft and hard EPS Geofoams depends on the specific requirements of the project and the level of support needed.</p></p>
