<p><p>The Food Biotechnology Market encompasses two major types: Transgenic Crops and Synthetic Biology Derived Products. Transgenic Crops are genetically modified crops that have been altered using biotechnology techniques to enhance their characteristics, such as resistance to pests or diseases. On the other hand, Synthetic Biology Derived Products are food products created through the use of synthetic biology techniques, which involve the design and construction of artificial genetic material to produce desired traits in organisms. These markets represent the application of biotechnology in the agricultural and food industries to improve crop productivity and create novel food products.</p></p>
