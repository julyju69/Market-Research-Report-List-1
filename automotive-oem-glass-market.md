<p><p>The automotive OEM glass market consists of three main types: tempered, laminated, and others. Tempered glass is treated with heat to increase its strength and shatter resistance. Laminated glass is made up of two or more layers with a plastic interlayer that holds the glass together when shattered. The "others" category typically includes technologies such as acoustic glass for noise reduction or heated glass for defrosting. These different types of glass cater to various needs and preferences of automakers and consumers.</p></p>
