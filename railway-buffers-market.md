<p><strong>The Railway Buffers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hydraulic Buffers</li><li>Friction Buffers</li><li>Rubber Buffers</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The railway buffers market consists of various types such as hydraulic buffers, friction buffers, rubber buffers, and others. Hydraulic buffers utilize hydraulic fluid to absorb and dissipate energy during train collisions, ensuring safety and minimizing damage. Friction buffers utilize a combination of materials and friction to absorb impact forces. Rubber buffers are made of rubber compounds and are designed to provide both impact absorption and energy dissipation. Other types of buffers include spring buffers, which utilize springs to absorb energy, and telescopic buffers, which can extend and retract to accommodate different train lengths.</p></p>
