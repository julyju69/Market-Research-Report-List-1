<p><p>Gourmet salt is available in various types, each with its unique characteristics and origins. Fleur De Sel, originating from coastal regions, is known for its delicate flavor and crunchy texture. Sel Gris, traditionally harvested from salt flats, possesses a grey color and a slightly moist texture. Himalayan Salt, mined from the Khewra Salt Mines in Pakistan, showcases a pink hue and is rich in minerals. Flake Salt is formed by evaporating seawater, resulting in thin, flat crystals and a mild taste. Specialty Salt encompasses diverse varieties created by infusing salts with different flavors, offering an array of taste options.</p></p>
