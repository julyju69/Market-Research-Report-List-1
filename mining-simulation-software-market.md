<p><p>The mining simulation software market can be categorized into two types: cloud-based mining simulation software and on-premise mining simulation software. Cloud-based software refers to simulations that are hosted on remote servers and accessed through the internet, allowing for flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness. Both types cater to the mining industry's need for virtual simulations to enhance training, planning, and decision-making processes.</p></p>
