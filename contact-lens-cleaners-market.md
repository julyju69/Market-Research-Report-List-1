<p><p>The contact lens cleaners market consists of two types: automatic cleaning and manual cleaning. Automatic cleaning type refers to cleaners that use advanced technology to clean and disinfect contact lenses, usually with the help of ultrasonic vibrations or hydrogen peroxide solution. These machines provide a quick and convenient way to clean lenses. On the other hand, manual cleaning type refers to cleaners that require users to manually clean their lenses by rubbing them with cleaning solution and rinsing them thoroughly. This traditional method allows for more control over the cleaning process but may take more time and effort.</p></p>
