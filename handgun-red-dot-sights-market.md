<p><strong>The Handgun Red Dot Sights Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SFP</li><li>FFP</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Handgun red dot sight market types can be categorized as SFP (Second Focal Plane) and FFP (First Focal Plane). In SFP sights, the reticle stays the same size regardless of the zoom level, resulting in a consistent aiming point. FFP sights, on the other hand, maintain the same reticle size in relation to the target as the zoom level changes. This enables precise aiming at various magnification levels. Both types have their advantages, with SFP offering simplicity and affordability, while FFP provides accuracy and versatility.</p></p>
