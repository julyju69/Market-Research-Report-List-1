There are two types of S-VDR market: Float Free Capsule S-VDR and Fixed Capsule S-VDR. A Float Free Capsule S-VDR is designed to automatically detach from a sinking ship and float to the surface, ensuring data retrieval even during emergencies. On the other hand, a Fixed Capsule S-VDR is permanently installed on the ship and does not detach.</p></p>
