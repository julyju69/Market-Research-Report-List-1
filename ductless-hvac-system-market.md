<p><p>A ductless HVAC system, also known as a mini-split system, is a type of heating, ventilation, and air conditioning system that does not require ductwork to distribute air throughout a building. Instead, it consists of an outdoor unit that houses the compressor and condenser, and one or more indoor units that are mounted on the walls or ceilings of individual rooms.</p><p>The market for ductless HVAC systems has been experiencing significant growth in recent years. This can be attributed to several factors such as increasing energy efficiency requirements, rising demand for personalized comfort control, and the need for flexible and easy installation options.</p><p>The global ductless HVAC system market is expected to grow at a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 14.5% during the forecast period. This growth can be attributed to the advantages offered by ductless systems, including energy efficiency, improved air quality, and individual temperature control.</p><p>One of the major trends in the ductless HVAC system market is the increasing adoption of smart and connected systems. With advancements in technology, ductless systems can now be integrated with smart home automation systems, allowing users to control temperature settings using their smartphones or voice assistants. This trend is expected to drive the market growth as consumers seek convenience and energy savings.</p><p>Another trend is the growing demand for multi-zone ductless systems. These systems enable different indoor units to be connected to a single outdoor unit, providing individual temperature control for multiple areas or zones within a building. This flexibility is particularly beneficial for commercial buildings, hotels, and large residential properties.</p><p>In conclusion, the ductless HVAC system market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years, driven by factors such as energy efficiency requirements, demand for personalized comfort control, and the increasing adoption of smart and connected systems.
