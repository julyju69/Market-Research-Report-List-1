<p><strong>The Automotive Engine Mineral Fluid Lubricants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Paraffinic Lubricant</li><li>Naphtenic Lubricant</li><li>Aromatic Lubricant</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive engine mineral fluid lubricants are categorized into three main types based on the composition of the base oil used: paraffinic lubricants, naphthenic lubricants, and aromatic lubricants. Paraffinic lubricants are derived from paraffin wax and are known for their stability and resistance to oxidation. Naphthenic lubricants are derived from naphthenic acids and are valued for their solvency and low pour point. Aromatic lubricants contain aromatic hydrocarbons and offer good thermal stability and high viscosity index. Each type of lubricant caters to specific engine requirements and operating conditions.</p></p>
