<p><strong>The Plastic Ball Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Threaded Connections</li><li>Flanged Connections</li><li>Butt Weld Connections</li><li>Socked Weld Connections</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Plastic ball valves are available in various types based on their connections. Threaded connections refer to valves with male or female threads that can be screwed onto the pipeline. Flanged connections involve valves with flanges that are bolted onto the pipeline. Butt weld connections utilize welding to connect the valve directly to the pipeline. Socked weld connections involve welding the valve into a socket on the pipeline. These different types cater to various installation needs and preferences in the plastic ball valves market.</p></p>
