<p><strong>The Automotive Fan Clutches Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-Thermal Fan Clutch</li><li>Thermal Fan Clutch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>There are two main types of fan clutches in the automotive market: non-thermal fan clutches and thermal fan clutches. </p><p>Non-thermal fan clutches operate solely based on the speed of the engine and maintain a constant fan speed. These clutches are relatively simple and cost-effective but may result in excessive fuel consumption or cooling under certain conditions.</p><p>Thermal fan clutches, on the other hand, use a temperature-sensitive coil or fluid to adjust the clutch engagement. This allows the fan to run faster or slower depending on the engine's cooling needs, resulting in better fuel efficiency and improved cooling performance.</p></p>
