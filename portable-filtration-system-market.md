<p><p>The Portable Filtration System market consists of three main types: </p><p>1. Pressure Filtration involves passing the liquid through a filter under high pressure, forcing it through the filter medium to separate impurities. </p><p>2. Centrifugal Filtration uses centrifugal force to separate solid particles from the liquid, as the mixture is spun at a high speed. </p><p>3. Vacuum Filtration utilizes a vacuum pump to draw the liquid through the filter, allowing the liquid to pass while retaining the solid particles. These methods offer efficient and portable solutions for filtration needs in various industries.</p></p>
