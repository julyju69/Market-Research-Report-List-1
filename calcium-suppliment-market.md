<p><p>Calcium supplements are dietary supplements that contain calcium to provide additional amounts of this essential mineral to the body. Calcium supplements come in various forms such as tablets, capsules, liquids, and chewable tablets.</p><p>The global calcium supplement market has witnessed significant growth in recent years and is projected to continue expanding at a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 14.3% during the forecast period. This growth can be attributed to several factors. Firstly, an increase in the prevalence of calcium deficiency and related disorders, such as osteoporosis, among the aging population is driving the demand for calcium supplements. Additionally, rising consumer awareness about the importance of maintaining good bone health and the role calcium plays in it is further fueling market growth.</p><p>Moreover, the growing popularity of functional foods and beverages fortified with calcium is also contributing to the market's expansion. The demand for these products is increasing as consumers seek convenient ways to meet their recommended daily calcium intake. Furthermore, the rise in vegan and lactose-intolerant populations has led to a surge in the sale of plant-based calcium supplements, catering to the specific needs of these consumer segments.</p><p>In terms of trends, there is a growing preference for calcium supplements that are free from artificial additives, allergens, and GMO ingredients. Furthermore, manufacturers are investing in research and development to introduce innovative delivery formats and formulations, such as calcium gummies and effervescent tablets, to enhance the user experience.</p><p>To sum up, the calcium supplement market is poised for steady growth due to the rising prevalence of calcium deficiency, increasing consumer awareness, and the development of innovative products to cater to evolving consumer demands.
