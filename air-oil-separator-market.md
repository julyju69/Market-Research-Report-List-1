<p><p>The Air Oil Separator market is categorized into three types: Pleated Air Oil Separator, Deep Filter Air Oil Separator, and Coalescing Air Oil Separator. Pleated Air Oil Separators are composed of pleated filter media that efficiently remove oil and contaminants from the air. Deep Filter Air Oil Separators have a deep filtration design to capture a larger volume of oil and impurities. Coalescing Air Oil Separators use special media to merge tiny oil droplets into larger ones, facilitating their separation from the air. All these separators ensure clean and contaminant-free air supply.</p></p>
