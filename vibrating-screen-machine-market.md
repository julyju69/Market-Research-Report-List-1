<p><p>The vibrating screen machine market can be divided into two types: free vibrating and constrained vibrating. In the free vibrating market, the screen machine operates with minimal restriction, allowing the material to move freely on the screen surface. However, in the constrained vibrating market, the screen machine applies constraints to the material, preventing it from freely moving on the surface. These two market types cater to different industries and applications, providing varying levels of control and efficiency in the screening process.</p></p>
