<p><ul><li>Single Core Cable</li><li>Multi Core Cable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Submarine power cables are classified into two types: single core cables and multi core cables. Single core cables consist of a single conductor surrounded by insulation and protective layers, offering high power transmission efficiency over long distances and deep ocean depths. On the other hand, multi core cables comprise multiple conductors bundled together, allowing for the transmission of multiple power circuits simultaneously. Both types play a crucial role in enabling the transmission of electricity between offshore wind farms, oil rigs, and mainland power grids, contributing to sustainable energy and connectivity.</p></p>
