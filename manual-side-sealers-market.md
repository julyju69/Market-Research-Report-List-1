<p><p>The Manual Side Sealers market consists of three main types: 2 Side Seal, 3 Side Seal, and 4 Side Seal. </p><p>2 Side Seal machines seal products on two sides, using heat and pressure. They are commonly used for packaging items like snacks and small electronic components.</p><p>3 Side Seal machines seal products on three sides, leaving one side open. These are often used for packaging items like food products, pharmaceuticals, and household goods.</p><p>4 Side Seal machines seal products on all four sides, creating a fully enclosed package. They are primarily used for packaging items that require extra protection, such as medical devices and sensitive electronics.</p></p>
