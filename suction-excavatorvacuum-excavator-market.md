<p><p>The suction excavator (vacuum excavator) market consists of three main types: liquid suctioning only, liquid and dry suctioning, and high velocity. In liquid suctioning only, the equipment is used solely for removing liquids. In liquid and dry suctioning, the machine is used to remove both liquid and dry materials, such as soil or debris. The high velocity market involves equipment with a powerful suction force capable of removing materials at a high speed. These variations cater to different excavation needs and preferences in the industry.</p></p>
