<p><p>The dish detergent market can be categorized into three types. Firstly, there are hand washing products, which are used for manual dish cleaning. These include dishwasher tablets, powders, and gels. Lastly, rinsing agents are used to enhance the effectiveness of dishwashing by reducing water spots and increasing the shine on dishes.
