<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Inline pH Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Combination pH Sensors</li><li>Differential pH Sensors</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Inline pH sensors are used in various industries to measure the acidity or alkalinity of liquids. There are different types of sensors available in the market. Combination pH sensors are the most common type, which combine a pH electrode and a reference electrode in one sensor. These sensors provide accurate pH measurements in a single unit. Differential pH sensors, on the other hand, measure the difference in pH between two different locations. This is useful for monitoring pH variations in different parts of a system. Other types of pH sensors include flat surface electrodes and non-glass sensors.</p></p>
