<p><p>Mag drive pumps, also known as magnetically driven pumps, are types of pumps that use the power of magnets to transmit torque from the motor to the impeller, eliminating the need for a traditional shaft seal. This eliminates the risk of leakage and is ideal for handling corrosive, toxic, or hazardous fluids. Mag drive pumps are widely used in industries such as chemicals, pharmaceuticals, water treatment, and oil & gas.</p><p>The current outlook for the mag drive pumps market is positive, with increasing demand for safer and more efficient pump solutions.
