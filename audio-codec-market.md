<p><p>The audio codec market is segmented into three types: mono-codec, stereo codec, and multi-channel codec. A mono-codec is designed for single-channel audio, where the sound is reproduced through a single speaker or headphone. A stereo codec supports two channels, creating a more immersive audio experience with separate left and right audio channels. On the other hand, a multi-channel codec is used for audio systems with more than two channels, such as surround sound systems, enabling a realistic audio environment with sound coming from different directions.</p></p>
