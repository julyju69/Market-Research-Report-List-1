<p><p>The under-display fingerprint module market consists of three main types: capacitive, optical, and ultrasonic. Capacitive fingerprint modules use electrical currents to create a fingerprint image. Optical modules use light to capture fingerprint details. Ultrasonic modules use sound waves to create a detailed fingerprint image. Each type has its own advantages and disadvantages in terms of accuracy, speed, and security. The choice of module type often depends on the specific requirements of the application and desired level of security.</p></p>
