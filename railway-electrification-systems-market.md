<p><p>The Railway Electrification Systems market consists of three main types. First is the Contact Lines System, where overhead wires are installed along the railway track to supply power to the trains through pantographs. The second is the Traction Power Supply System, which provides the necessary electrical power to the contact lines. Lastly, the Others market includes various supplementary systems and components such as substations, transformers, and control systems that support the effective functioning of the railway electrification systems. These systems collectively enable trains to operate efficiently and smoothly.</p></p>
